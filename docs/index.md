# Product Management School
From Zero → FAANG-Level Product Manager

## Core Question
How do you discover the right problems, make better product decisions, and lead teams to build products customers love?

---

## Course Overview
By the end of this course you should be able to

- ✅ Think like a PM
- ✅ Speak the language of engineers
- ✅ Analyze products with data
- ✅ Write industry-quality PRDs
- ✅ Prioritize features confidently
- ✅ Lead cross-functional discussions
- ✅ Present recommendations backed by evidence

---

## The PM Operating System
Everything revolves around one loop:

Observe → Understand → Decide → Build → Measure → Learn → Repeat

Every module teaches one stage of this loop.

---

## Modules

### MODULE 1 — Product Thinking
**Big Question**
What problem are we actually solving?

**Topics Covered**
- What is Product Management?
- Product vs Project Management
- Product Lifecycle
- Jobs-to-be-Done
- Customer Obsession
- Product Strategy
- Product Vision
- Product-Market Fit

**Key Frameworks**
Product Pyramid: Vision → Strategy → Goals → Roadmap → Features

**Practice**
Choose one product and answer:
- Who is the user?
- What problem?
- Current solution?
- Better solution?
- Why now?

---

### MODULE 2 — Customer Discovery
**Big Question**
How do we know we're solving the right problem?

**Topics**
- Customer interviews
- Surveys
- User personas
- Journey maps
- JTBD interviews
- Pain points
- Opportunity mapping

**Framework**
Observe → Interview → Patterns → Insights → Problem Statement

**Practice**
- Interview 5 users
- Write top 10 frustrations

---

### MODULE 3 — Product Analytics
**Big Question**
How do we know the product is healthy?

**Topics**
- North Star Metric
- KPIs, Funnels, Retention, Engagement, Activation, Churn
- LTV, CAC
- SQL basics
- Experimentation

**Framework**
AARRR Funnel: Acquire → Activate → Retain → Revenue → Referral

**Practice**
Analyze Robinhood onboarding: Where do users drop? Why?

---

### MODULE 4 — Product Strategy
**Big Question**
What should we build?

**Topics**
- Market sizing
- Competitive analysis
- SWOT, Porter's Five Forces, Blue Ocean
- Network effects, Growth loops
- Business model & Unit economics

**Framework**
Strategy Stack: Mission → Market → Users → Problems → Solutions → Business Model

**Practice**
Can Robinhood expand into teen investing? Support or reject — why?

---

### MODULE 5 — Prioritization
**Big Question**
What should we build first?

**Topics**
- RICE, ICE, MoSCoW, Kano
- Cost vs Value
- Opportunity scoring
- Risk analysis

**Framework**
Impact → Confidence → Effort → Risk

**Practice**
Rank 10 feature ideas

---

### MODULE 6 — Technical Foundations
**Big Question**
How do engineers think?

**Topics**
- Frontend, Backend, API, Database
- Authentication, Cloud, AI, Machine Learning
- Caching, Latency, Scalability

**Need to understand — not necessarily code.**

**Practice**
Draw Robinhood architecture: User → API → Database → Portfolio

### MODULE 7 — Product Design
**Big Question**
What creates delightful products?

**Topics**
- UX, UI, Accessibility, Design systems
- Information architecture, Wireframes, Figma, User flows

**Framework**
Discover → Define → Design → Prototype → Test

**Practice**
Redesign Robinhood's watchlist

### MODULE 8 — Execution
**Big Question**
How do products actually get built?

**Topics**
- Agile, Scrum, Sprint, Kanban
- Story points, Backlog, User stories
- Standups, Release planning, Risk management

**Framework**
Sprint: Plan → Build → Review → Retrospective

**Practice**
Run one-week sprint

---

### MODULE 9 — Communication
**Big Question**
How do PMs influence without authority?

**Topics**
- PRDs, Executive summaries, Presentations
- Stakeholder management, Conflict resolution
- Meeting facilitation, Decision documents, Storytelling

**Framework**
PREP: Point → Reason → Evidence → Point

**Practice**
Present one feature recommendation

---

### MODULE 10 — Leadership
**Big Question**
How do PMs lead without being managers?

**Topics**
- Ownership, Delegation, Influence, Negotiation
- Coaching, Managing ambiguity, Decision making
- Cross-functional leadership

**Framework**
Situational Leadership

**Practice**
Resolve engineering vs design disagreement

---

### MODULE 11 — AI Product Management
**Big Question**
How do AI products differ from traditional software?

**Topics**

- Hallucinations, Latency, AI UX
- Guardrails, Human-in-the-loop

**Practice**

---

### MODULE 12 — Product Sense (Capstone)
**Big Question**
Can you think like a senior PM?

**Case Studies**
Robinhood, Airbnb, Spotify, Uber, Duolingo, Notion, Stripe

**Weekly Challenge**
Pick one product → Find one problem → Research users → Analyze data → Generate ideas → Prioritize → Write PRD → Present recommendation → Reflect

## The PM Mental Models Library
Rather than memorizing frameworks, build a toolbox of thinking models.

Category | Models
---|---
Decision Making | OODA Loop, First Principles, Inversion, Expected Value, Regret Minimization
Product Strategy | JTBD, Product-Market Fit, Blue Ocean, Flywheel, Network Effects
Customer Understanding | Empathy Mapping, Customer Journey Mapping, Kano Model
Prioritization | RICE, ICE, MoSCoW, Eisenhower Matrix
Analytics | AARRR Funnel, HEART Framework, Cohort Analysis, Funnel Analysis
Execution | Agile, Scrum, Kanban, OKRs
Communication | Pyramid Principle, PREP, Storytelling with Data
